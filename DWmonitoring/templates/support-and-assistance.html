FAQS

What should I do if I find suspicious activity ?
If you encounter suspicious activity, please report it immediately by clicking the 'Report Suspicious Activity' button within the application. Provide as much detail as possible to help us investigate.
How does dark web monitoring work?
Our application scans various dark web sources, forums, and marketplaces for specified keywords, such as your email address, company name, or other sensitive information. When a match is found, you receive an alert with details of the detected activity.
Is my data safe with DWM application?
Absolutely. We prioritize your data security and privacy. All monitored data is encrypted and handled according to our strict privacy policy.
Can I share my monitoring results with others?
Yes, you can export your monitoring results and share them with others. Go to the 'Reports' section, select the data you want to share, and click 'Export'. You can download the pdf file.
